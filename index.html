Welcome to Nova's Legacy

Saving Cheetah's One Spot at a Time

Nova’s Legacy is a large wildlife conservation reserve located in Bela-Bela, Limpopo. While our primary focus is on Cheetah Conservation and Breeding, we have a range of other animals you can experience one-on-one.
